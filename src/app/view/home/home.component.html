Why "NightinGale"?

"It was the nightingale and not the lark" – in Shakespeare’s Romeo and Juliet the nightingale plays an important role. With its powerful and beautiful singing messages are spread. That’s what we do!

But do you know Florence Nighingale, the patron of our idea?

She was a British nurse in the 19th century - Known as “the Lady with the lamp”. She revolutionized the organization of the nursing care. The nurse profession gained great prestige through her tireless commitment.

That sounds familiar to our situation these days, doesn’t it?

As if that were not enough, she brought her passion for mathematics, and statistics in particular, into the field of health care and carried out analyses, for instance, with regard to hygienic conditions.

As the pioneer of data visualization Florence Nightingale is the perfect patron for our idea!
